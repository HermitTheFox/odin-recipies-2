Biscuits

Description

Simple homemade biscuit recipie. Made with common ingredients. Perfect to use in/on pot pie, chicken and biscuits, etc. Slightly denser biscuit with a touch of sweetness. Perfect for pairing with traditional southern comfort food.

Will need a floured surface, mixing bowl, grater, fork, rolling pin, and cookie/biscuit cutter. Prep time is about 15 mins and cook time is 20-25 mins. In order to have optimal biscuit texture, place butter in the freezer 15 to 20 mins before use. This will ensure the best consitency for your dough.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 6 Tbsp very cold Salted Butter
  • 3/4 cup Whole Milk

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar together.
  2. Grate butter and work it into the dry mix with your hands.
  3. Add milk and stir gently with a fork until fully incorporated.
  4. Turn dough out onto a clean floured surface. Fold it over a few times, and then roll it out with your rolling pin to get a 1 to 2 inch thick layer.
  5. Cut 6-10(or as many as you can get) biscuits from the dough.
  6. Place biscuits on baking sheet or on top of pot pie mix.
  7. Cook for 20 mins or until golden brown.
