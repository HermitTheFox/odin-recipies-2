Green Beans

Description

Very easy southern style green bean recipe. Incorporates caramelized red onins and fresh bacon bits for a burst of flavor. Pairs very well as a side with most any dish. Can also be enjoyed as a snack or small meal.

Will need a sharp knife, cutting board, stove(microwave will also work), and a large cooking sheet. Prep time is about 10- 15 mins. Cook time is 20 to 25 mins. You may want to substitute the granulated garlic for fresh minced garlic, as this will give the green beans a more aromatic flavor. See ingredients below.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 Red Onion Julienned Click link for julienne cut tutorial Julienne Tutorial
  • 1 cup fresh chopped Bacon Bits
  • 2.5 lbs Whole Green Beans
  • 2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Granulated Garlic or 2-3 cloves freshly miced Garlic. See reference above.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Inspect green beans and remove any stems or bad beans. Rinse thoroughly.
  3. On cutting board, julienne your 1/2 red onion and chop your bacon bits. This would also be the point when you mince your garlic if you have elected to do so.
  4. Add bacon, onion, beans,and fresh garlic(optional) to large tub or bowl.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and granulated garlic (if fresh garlic not used)
  6. Melt butter using pan on stovetop with low to medium heat. You can also use a microwave with a safe dish.
  7. Add melted butter to green bean mixture and mix thoroughly.
  8. Place mixture on large cooking sheet and spread out evenly.
  9. Bake at 350 for 20 -25 mins, or until beans are desired texture.
