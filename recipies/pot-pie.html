Pot Pie

Description

Traditional southern pot pie recipe. This recipe is made from scratch using common ingriedents. About 1 hour of prep time, and 20-25 mins to cook.

Will need a large pot, a stove, an oven, a whisk, and a large spoon or ladel. We will also be making a roux, which requires constant attention.NEVER LEAVE YOUR ROUX UNATTENDED! Top with biscuit before baking.(You can use canned biscuits or make them from scratch. Link to homemade biscuits here. Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp Salted Butter
  • A few sprigs of Thyme or a 1/4 Tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 small Onion chopped
  • 3 Carrots peeled and chopped
  • 3 Celery stalks chopped
  • 1/4 cup Flour
  • 2 cups Whole Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups Chicken Broth
  • 4 cups shredded Chicken
  • 2 cups frozen Peas or cooked Green Beans
  • 2 cups cooked Corn
  • 3 Tbsp freshly cut Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in skillet over medium to low heat.
  3. Add the onion and thyme. Cook for about 5 mins.
  4. Add parsley, celery, and carrots. Cook until carrots and celery are tender, about 15 mins.
  5. Stir in flour very slowly and continuously. About 5 mins. This will create a roux.
  6. Gradually stir in milk continuously. 3-5 mins.
  7. Stir in chicken broth slowly until fully incorporated.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Transfer mix into baking dish.
  10. Add corn, peas/green beans, and shredded chicken. Stir into mixture.
  11. Add biscuits on top.
  12. Bake for about 25 mins or until biscuits are golden brown.
